Data_Processing_Addendum_GammaHealth - Contract Document

1. Parties
This Agreement is entered into between Customer and Supplier effective on the Effective Date.

2. Definitions
‘Services’ means the services described in Schedule A.

Indemnity: Processor indemnifies Customer for all third-party claims arising from any data incident, without a monetary cap.

Security: ISO 27001 aligned controls; encryption at rest and in transit.

Governing Law: Republic of Ireland.

Retention: Data retained for 90 days after termination then securely deleted.

Data Protection: GDPR Article 28 requirements. Subprocessors require prior written authorisation. 72-hour breach notification.

Schedule A: Services
The Supplier shall provide the Services as described in the Order Form.
